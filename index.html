About Me

Hello! I’m Juan Avila and I’m a recent graduate from Kalamazoo College (Go Hornets!). Although I attended college in Michigan, I am a born-and-raised native from the Mission District of San Francisco. During my time in high school, I was supported by nonprofit organizations that helped me get my first job and even navigate the challenges of being a first-generation college student. I’ve been able to explore my interest in nonprofit work as a consultant intern at EducationSuperHighway, an ed-tech nonprofit organization, and as an operations intern at Hopelab, a nonprofit creating technology-based solutions to improve youth wellness. Through these experiences, I’ve gained a greater interest in technology, so I am currently taking classes on technology and programming at Dev/Mission. Once I finish these classes, I will seek an IT or programming-related internship to put the skills I gain into practice, so that I may advance my understanding of technology and be one step closer to a career at a tech nonprofit.
